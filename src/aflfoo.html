Football Academy

1. Mainland Football Academy



Regarded as one of the best football academies in Kolkata, the Sambaran Football Academy was established by former Bengal footballer, Sambaran Banerjee. With more than 20 coaches for different age groups, they organize the largest Under-14 football tournaments in the state.

Centre: Jatin Bagchi Road, Southern Park, lake Terrace, Lake Gardens, Kolkata, West Bengal 700029

Age Group: 6 years and above

Fees Structure: Admission fee RS 500/-, monthly RS 150/-

Facilities: Gloves, 2 Shoes, 3 Dress, 5 Nets, Physical Training, Technical assistance

Days and Timings: Monday to Saturday (7 am to 5 pm)

Contact: Mr. Sambaran Banerjee (+91 3324639662, +91 6296110860)





2. Calcutta Football Academy




Calcutta Football Academy is the best place to ace your skills. They provide individual coaching sessions to make sure that the kids are able to learn football from scratch or polish their football abilities.

The sessions are mostly an hour and a half long, conducted by the former students of the academy. The academy was established by Abdul Masood, who is an ECB and BCCI Level certified coach. They also organize overseas tours to countries like Australia, England, and South Africa

Centre: Vivekananda Park (West) Ground Opposite: Kamala Girl’s High School, Kolkata, West Bengal 700029

Age Group: N/A

Fees Structure: On Request

Facilities: N/A

Days and Timings: Saturday to Sunday (Morning and Afternoon); Thursday (Afternoon)

Contact: Mr. Abdul Masood (+91 9831041010)
